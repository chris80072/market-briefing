# Taiwan Stock Pre-Market Briefing

Daily compilation of international news and market information after US stock market close, providing reference for Taiwan stock investment decisions.

## 📋 Report Contents

Each Taiwan stock pre-market briefing includes the following sections:

### 1. US and International Market Close Overview
- US stock trio (Dow Jones, S&P 500, Nasdaq)
- Philadelphia Semiconductor Index (SOX)
- Major ADRs (TSMC, UMC, ASX, etc.)
- 10Y US Treasury yield, US Dollar Index, VIX
- Oil prices, gold, copper and other commodities
- USD/TWD exchange rate

### 2. Five Key Points and Impact Analysis
Selected five pieces of information most likely to significantly impact Taiwan stocks, each including:
- **Source** — Original news source link
- **Event Summary** — What happened (with specific figures and sources)
- **Impact on Taiwan Stocks** — Which sectors/stocks affected and direction
- **Risk Warnings** — Follow-up risks to monitor
- **Investment Recommendations** — Trading direction and strategy

### 3. Taiwan Stock Focus Points
- **Large-Cap Focus** — TSMC, Foxconn and major holdings
- **Sector Rotation Observation** — Strong/weak/opportunity sectors
- **Technical and Fund Position Analysis** — Key support levels, foreign investor flows, margin balances
- **Important Events Calendar** — Daily and upcoming announcements, earnings, economic data

### 4. Conclusions and Trading Recommendations
- **Opening Forecast** — Bullish/bearish/sideways with reasoning
- **Strategy Recommendations** — Categorized by investment style (conservative/swing/long-term)
- **Risk Events to Watch** — Items to monitor in next 24-72 hours

### 5. Data Verification Notes
- Source attribution for all figures
- Items that cannot be verified marked as "Awaiting Confirmation"

## 🎯 How to Use

1. **Search by date** — Each report named `YYYY-MM-DD.md`
2. **Browse by month** — Navigate to `year/month/` folder to see all reports for that month
3. **Quick lookup** — Use GitHub search feature to find specific topics or stocks

## ⏰ Update Schedule

- One report generated for each Taiwan stock trading day
- Usually generated after US market close (Taiwan time next morning)
- If Taiwan market is closed, report perspective adjusted to "next opening day"
