# US Stock Market Briefing

Daily compilation of US and global market key information, market dynamics, and investment analysis, providing reference for US stock investment decisions.

## 📋 Report Contents

Each US stock market briefing includes the following sections:

### 1. US and Global Market Close Overview
- US stock trio (Dow Jones, S&P 500, Nasdaq) closing prices and changes
- Dow Transportation Index, Russell 2000 Small-cap Index
- Tech stocks (Nasdaq-100), S&P 500 Growth performance
- International stock markets (Japan, UK, Europe, etc.)
- US 10Y Treasury yield, high-yield bond spreads
- US Dollar Index, major currency pairs
- VIX volatility index, commodity futures (oil, gold, copper)
- Cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum)

### 2. Five Key Points and Market Analysis
Selected five most influential pieces of information, each including:
- **Source** — Original news source link
- **Event Summary** — What happened and market context
- **Market Impact** — Effects on different sectors and major stocks
- **Risk Assessment** — Follow-up developments and risk factors to monitor
- **Investment Recommendations** — Strategy adjustments and risk management suggestions

### 3. US Stock Sectors and Individual Stocks Observation
- **Strong Sectors** — Leading performing industries
- **Weak Sectors** — Industries under pressure
- **Key Stocks** — Major tech, financial, and industrial stocks movement
- **Technical Levels** — Key support and resistance for major indices
- **Fundamentals** — Important earnings, economic data, corporate developments

### 4. Federal Reserve and Central Bank Dynamics
- Interest rate decisions and policy outlook
- Major central bank officials' remarks
- Market expectations for future policies
- Inflation and employment data interpretation

### 5. Geopolitics and Macroeconomic Analysis
- Global trade and geopolitical events
- Energy and commodity price movements
- Emerging markets and currency dynamics
- Economic data and market expectations gaps

### 6. Conclusions and Trading Recommendations
- **Market Outlook** — Short-term and medium-term prospects
- **Risk Assessment** — Main downside risks
- **Investment Strategy** — Asset allocation and stock recommendations
- **Items to Watch** — Key future dates and events

3. **Track specific topics** — Use GitHub search to find specific central banks, geopolitical events, or stocks

## ⏰ Update Schedule

- One report generated for each US stock trading day
- Usually generated after US market close (4 PM ET)
- If US market is closed, report explains closure reason and next opening date
