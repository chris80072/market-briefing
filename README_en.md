# Market Briefing

Daily compilation of key information, technical analysis, and investment recommendations for Taiwan stock market and US stock market.

## 📂 Directory Structure

- **`tw_stocks/`** — Taiwan Stock Pre-Market Briefing
  - Daily compilation of international news and market information after US stock market close
  - Five key points most likely to significantly impact Taiwan stocks
  - Technical analysis, fund positioning analysis, and trading recommendations

- **`us_stocks/`** — US Stock Market Briefing
  - US and global market price overview
  - Major financial news and geopolitical event analysis
  - Market outlook and investment strategy recommendations

## 📅 Update Frequency

- **New report generated for each trading day**
- **Organized by year → month → date** (`YYYY/MM/YYYY-MM-DD.md`)

## 🗂️ Usage

```
market-briefing/
```

## 📝 Report Contents

Each briefing includes the following main sections:

1. **Market Close Overview** — Major indices, exchange rates, commodity prices, etc.
2. **Five Key Points Analysis** — Most impactful market events and analysis
3. **Taiwan/US Stock Focus** — Sector rotation, technical levels, important events
4. **Conclusions and Trading Recommendations** — Opening outlook and strategy suggestions
5. **Data Verification Notes** — Information sources and verification status
